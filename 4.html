Acer Chromebook CB5-571-C1DZ (15.6-inch Full HD, Intel Celeron, 4GB, 16GB)

Think Bigger

The Acer Chromebook 15 is the biggest thing to happen to the Chromebook. It has a number of unique and defining features: it is the World's first Chromebook with a 15.6-inch Full HD wide viewing angle display, the first Chromebook with the latest Intel processor based on Broadwell micro-architecture, and it uses the new dual-band 802.11ac wireless technology. It all adds up to the right mix of features to make you think bigger and reimagine whats possible with a Chromebook.

  • Intel Celeron Dual-Core Processor 3205U (1.5GHz, 2MB L3 Cache)
  • Google Chrome Operating System
  • 15.6" Full HD Widescreen LED-backlit Display (1920 x 1080 resolution, 16:9 aspect ratio)
  • 4GB DDR3L SDRAM Memory
  • 16GB SSD
  • Intel HD Graphics
  • High-Definition Audio Support
  • Upward-Facing Dual-Stereo Speakers
  • Secure Digital (SD) card reader
  • 802.11ac WiFi featuring MIMO technology (Dual-Band 2.4GHz and 5GHz)
  • Bluetooth 4.0
  • 1 - USB 3.0 Port
  • 1 - USB 2.0 Port
  • 1 - HDMI port with HDCP Support
  • 4-Cell Lithium Ion Battery (3220 mAh, 48Whr)
  • Up to 9-hours Battery Life

The Right Size

Acer expands upon its already dominant Chromebook position with the Worlds first 15.6-inch Full HD Chromebook letting you see more, do more and explore more than ever before. With a 27% larger screen area than the 13.3-inch Chromebook, and a 24% larger screen than a 14-inch Chromebook means more content can be shown and less scrolling and productivity tools such as Excel are easier to interact with. In short, its just the right size for you to make this Chromebook your 'everything' book.

The smart design of the Acer Chromebook 15 brings videos and games to the next level with a stunning large-scale display. The display is bright and crisp with a 1920 x 1080 Full HD resolution with wide viewing angle display, allowing you to share content simply and easily with those around you. From bedtime storytelling to watching a movie, youll experience a consistent color and viewing experience from any perspective. And with its high-dynamic-range imaging (HDR) web cam which provides clearer photos and supports better Google Hangouts experience in HD, you can feel closer to family and friends with crystal clear high resolution video chats, presenting you at your best, too.

Experience your entertainment in all of its glory. Two full-sized upward facing speakers bring movies to life, give songs more depth and make games more engaging and immersive.

The Right Speed

In addition to having the largest screen of any Chromebook, the Acer Chromebook 15 is incredibly fast. Powered by the latest Intel processor based on Broadwell micro-architecture, the Acer Chromebook 15 blows away anything youve seen before. Thanks to that, and the solid-state drive, the Acer Chromebook 15 fully boots up in 7 seconds and smoothly powers through tough tasks and provides quick access to stored files, movies, songs and photos.

The Acer Chromebook 15 brings speed to another level. Equipped with the latest 802.11ac wireless featuring MIMO technology, experience up to 3x faster performance when your Chromebook is connected to an 802.11ac based router making everything from online gaming to streaming video both faster and more reliable.

With a long-lasting battery life, the Chromebook 15 keeps the full Chrome OS experience at your fingertips. Up to 9 hours of battery life helps you power through both work and play. With more battery life and mobility than competitors, you can extend your productivity and enjoy life on-the-go.

Store and access your photos, music, videos, documents and more from anywhere with Google Drive. Its simple, and all your files are backed up automatically online, safely and securely. Plus enjoy 100 GB of free storage on Google Drive for two years.

The Right Feel

The Acer Chromebook 15 is big enough and fast enough for all-day use, so its also been designed to withstand heavy daily use and feel great all day. The durable fabric-like surface provides a premium feel that also maximizes grip, while the anti-glare Acer ComfyView display doesnt strain the eyes, even after long hours of use. Its a Chromebook that looks and feels great on your desk, on your lap, or on the go.

The Chromebook 15 is one of the slimmest Chromebooks on the market. Its ultra-thin Full HD 15.6-inch frame makes it easy to slip into a purse, backpack or brief case and take along with you. The touchpad the largest of any Chromebook provides plenty of room to scroll and gesture freely and effortlessly.

Watch movies, play games or get work done (if you really have to). The Chrome Web Store offers thousands of free apps, themes, and extensions to help you make the most of your Chromebook. Plus, the Chromebook will keep it all fresh with automatic updates.
